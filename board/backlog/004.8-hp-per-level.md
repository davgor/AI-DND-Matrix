# HP-per-level formula

## Description
Implement HP accumulation per level: archetype hit die (fixed average, not rolled) + Body modifier, accumulated across levels.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] `computeHP(archetype, level, bodyScore)` is deterministic (no RNG) and accumulates correctly across levels
- [ ] Unit tested for at least 2 distinct archetypes (different hit die sizes) across several levels

# End-to-end journal smoke test

## Description
Validate the full journal loop in a real running app: play through a major beat (e.g. completing a quest, a notable NPC encounter) and confirm a character-voiced entry appears on the sheet, while routine/combat turns do not spam new entries.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Playing through a major story beat results in a new journal entry written in informal first-person voice appearing on the character sheet
- [ ] Playing through routine exploration/combat turns does not produce a flood of journal entries
- [ ] Restarting the app preserves journal entries and their order
- [ ] Opening the character sheet for a character with no journal entries yet shows a clean empty state
